# Why I Built This

In my first year of teaching, I quickly learned that the challenges I was navigating were never just one thing at a time.

A student was confused about fractions — **and** acting out — **and** missing from the gradebook.

I was trying to teach, coach, correct behavior, track progress, and reach families — all at once, with tools that didn't talk to each other.

**Teachers deserve better. Students deserve better. This is my attempt to build it.**

# The Problem — In One Sentence

**Every minute a student sits in confusion without support is a minute they fall further behind — academically, behaviorally, and emotionally.**

And in most classrooms, there is no system designed to close that gap *in real time.*

~30 seconds. Pause after the first sentence. Let it breathe.

# Who This Happens To Most

The students who most need to be reached are the ones least likely to raise their hand.

They don't signal confusion. They go quiet. They disengage. Then they escalate.

By the time we have data — a quiz score, a behavior referral, a missed assignment — **the moment is already gone.**

This is not a failure of teachers. It is a failure of the tools they've been given.

# What Class Pulse Does — The Full Picture

Class Pulse is a classroom intelligence platform built around **five interconnected systems:**

1. 🟢 **Comprehension Pulse** — real-time student confusion signals, anonymous and live
2. 🧠 **AI Instructional Coach** — standards-aligned intervention script in under 30 seconds
3. 📋 **Behavior Ladder** — structured consequence tracking with auto parent notifications
4. 💵 **RAM Buck Economy** — voice-operated student incentive system, earned through academics and behavior
5. 📒 **Gradebook + CFU Tracker** — daily check-for-understanding logging with absence alerts

**One platform. The full classroom. Every minute of the day.**

# For the Student — What Changes

- A student signals confusion *without raising her hand* — anonymously, dignified
- She earns RAM Bucks for a correct answer, for mastering a standard, for a great day
- Her behavior is tracked fairly and consistently — and her family is kept in the loop
- Her teacher knows exactly where she is — academically and emotionally — before the bell rings

**She is no longer invisible.**

# For the Teacher — What Changes

- Real-time visibility into *who is lost right now* — during the lesson, not after
- A 30-second coaching script tied to the exact FL BEST standard being taught
- Voice commands to award RAM Bucks, log behavior, and note iReady progress — hands-free
- Post-class reports with comprehension timelines, mastery grids, and differentiation groups
- A gradebook that alerts when entries are missing before the day is over

**The cognitive load doesn't disappear. But the tool carries more of it.**

# What We Found

When teachers had Class Pulse during instruction:

- **80%+** of students actively used the comprehension signal by Week 4
- **30% more** Checks for Understanding were completed daily
- Average intervention time dropped from **next day → under 60 seconds**
- Teachers reported delivering targeted support an average of **8× per class period** that previously went unaddressed

> *"For the first time, I feel like I didn't leave anyone behind today."*
